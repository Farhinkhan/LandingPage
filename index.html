Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know.

How does the app work?

The app uses GPS, Wi-Fi, or cellular networks to determine the location of a device or asset and updates the information in real time.

Is an internet connection required to use the app?

Yes, an internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data) is required for real-time tracking. Some features may work offline and sync later.

Can I track multiple devices or users?

Yes, you can add multiple devices or users under your account, depending on your subscription plan.

How accurate is the location tracking?

The accuracy depends on GPS signal strength, network connectivity, and environmental factors (e.g., buildings or tunnels).

Is the app free to use?

The app may have a free version with limited features. A premium plan may offer advanced features like real-time tracking and history storage.
